HOTEL THAMEL

Located centrally in the popular tourist area, Hotel Thamel offers a homely and friendly environment to make anyone feel like their own home. The name of the hotel is itself taken from the place this hotel is located.

Hotel Thamel is 15 mins drive from the airport and is in walking distance from most of the attractions like Monkey Temple, Garden of Dreams, King Palace, and Durbar Square.

Hotel Thamel is a perfect place for budget travelers. The hotel is regularly visited by international travelers, thanks to its convenient location.

The Hotel Thamel is well equipped with rentable trekking accessories, wildlife tours, rafting, and other sightseeing tours which can all be arranged at the reception area.
